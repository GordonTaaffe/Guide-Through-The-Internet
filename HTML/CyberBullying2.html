What is Bullying?

Bullying is unwanted attention


Types of Bullying:

  • Physical Bullying- Harm to the Body.

  • Verbal Bullying- Harm Said Which Effects you Mentally such as 'Am I Good Enough' is an example of mental bullying that you ask yourself

  • Cyber Bullying- Harm said or shown over the internet. Also the main topic we will be talking about


Is Bullying Acceptable? No Bullying is never Acceptable as you are causing harm to others.

What Should you do if you are Being Bullied? Tell a friend, family or teacher. It helps more to tell an adult.

What Should you do if you see someone being bullied. Tell a parent, friend or teacher about it and try to comfort them.
